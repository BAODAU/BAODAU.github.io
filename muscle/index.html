Muscle

i can build muscle in a calorie deficit

build muscle:

  • water
  • protein: 1g of protein per pound of weight
  • energy: 3500 cal/lb of fat
  • training stimulus:

training tips

  • warm up with light cardio. should be sweating a little bit before starting.

    train the hardest exercises first

  • squats

  • compounds first then isolation

time your set

  • time under tension is more important:40 seconds for hypertrophy

work rest ratio

  • 3:1 or 5:1. rest should be 3-5 times longer than work time

use proper form

train muscle groups twice a week

  • get the volume in there
  • focus on weak body parts
  • small muscle groups can handle 15-20 sets a week
  • 7-10 sets per session
  • bigger muscle groups can deal with 20-30 sets

stretching

  • done after workout
