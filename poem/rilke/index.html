I am to alone in the world, and not alone enough to make every moment holy I am too tiny in this world, and not tiny enough just to lie before you like a thing, shrewd and secretive I want my own will, and I want simply to be with my will, as it goes toward action, and in the silent, sometimes hardly moving times when something is coming near
