Life is not fair#

  • there are factors and events beyond your control that will impact how your life plays out.
  • by putting yourself in a position to succeed, you maximize the chances you will achieve whatever it is that you want.

Review of how I’m spending my Time#

Awareness#

  • an awareness of how you experience time can tell you a lot about yourself
  • Objective: balance your consumption of time in a way that meshes goals and passions.
  • keeping a journal sensitizes you as to how you are spending your depreciating asset: the hours of your life
  • if you do not have an awareness of how you are spending time, you have no ability to manage your time effectively

Goals#

What are your goals?

What do you hope to accomplish in the short term?

What do you want your life to look like in the long term?

How do you plan on getting from where you are to where you want to be?

What are the steps from here to there?

Goals provide the road map#

  • By thinking about steps and time, you engage your analytical and creative juices. You have a better understanding of what you have to do to get where you want to be.

  • You are starting to plan. You are starting to actually visualize the achievment of your goals. Visualization increases desire which increases intensity.

  • You are testing your resolve. By identifying specific steps and time requirements, you double check your intensity for certain goals.

Choices#

  • You need to find a balance between living for the moment: doing exactly what you want to do right now. And living for the future: putting yourself in a position to lead the life you want at some point down the road.
  • time management is in large part about choices. calls from friends. the desire to sleep late. commitments and obligations. seduction of the internet.

Successful people do not enjoy doing the things that others do not. Successful people simply do them nevertheless.

  • by constantly reflecting on your goals and readjusting at times,you increase the probability that your life will proceed on your agenda, and not someone else’s or by fate.

The secret of success is learning how to use pain and pleasure instead of having pain and pleasure use you. If you do that, you’re in control of your life. If you don’t, life controls you.

Using the hours you have to maximum effectiveness#

  • not all hours are created equal
  • no magic: exercise daily, eat healthy, get lots of fluids, moderate your intake of caffeine and alcohol.
  • use the time when you have the most energy. The power of Full engagement

create time#

  • downtime periods: times when you are waiting for something to happen and when you you are cooling your heels.

minimize time waste#

  • make decisions quickly
  • speed reading
  • improve memorization skills

improve memorization skills#

  • stretch your mind: do activities that forces you to push yourself mentally
  • spread out your intake of food and drink: when you overload yourself with food and/or drink, your sugar level spikes. and then shortly drops dramatically.
  • exercise: increases blood flow to the brain
  • antioxidants: berries, broccoli, spinach.
  • learn memory skills,
  • perform associations/rhymes
  • get enough sleep
  • concentrate
  • convert important facts into a format that assists your recall.
  • dont stress about memory skills

procrastination#

  • result of a powerful law: INERTIA
  • in order to achieve forward motion, people need to push themselves

strategies to defeat procrastination#

  • keep a piece of paper or index card in your pocket with your goals written in bold ink:
  • break big projects up into manageable pieces: most of us are overwhelmed when contemplating a huge project.
  • train your mind to recognize the first signs of laziness or procrastination:
  • always carry a means of taking notes: putting your thoughts in writing will help propel you into action the next chance you have to get started

use INERTIA to your advantage#

  • people who develop the mental strength and fortitude to deal with procrastination and the other challenges we all face are most likely to achieve their dreams

  • those who develop mental strength are more likely to:

          1. keep on schedule/plan
      2. make appropriate choices
      3. maintain good focus
      4. defeat procrastination

develop strong mental fortitude#

  • you are not your mind. you have dominion over negative thoughts in your head: read The power of now

    The single most important step you can take is to learn to disidentify from you mind. At some point you will smile at the thoughts in your mind, as if they are antics of a small child.
