If you lack the courage to live the life you really wnt, you’re going to be chronically disappointed in yourself

keep my promises to myself#

  • people with low self-esteem are very good at keeping their promises to other people and very bad at keeping promises to themselves
  • choose good career path because society admires
  • choose to marry someone because they know their family would approve
  • true self-esteem comes from balancing the wants and needs of others with the wants and needs of myself
  • i need a solid founation of keeping promises to myself

spend more time with people i genuiunely like#

  • i am the average of the five people i spend the most time with

  • i need to hang around bright curious passionate people, not those with negative traits

  • the people we spend time with affect our wellbeing and sense of self

  • if i hang around with people who don’t like me, it’s going to be harder to like myself

be gentle in the way i talk to myself#

  • self-talk is a behavior: it’s something we do so often that it becomes a habit
  • best way to undo a habit of overly negative self-talk is to focus on gentleness

make time to do things i actually enjoy#
