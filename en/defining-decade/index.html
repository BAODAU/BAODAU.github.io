experiences#

  • high school and our twenties are the only time when we have the most self-defining memories

  • adolescence is the time of our first attempt to form life stores. the stories we tell about ourselves become facets of our identity. they say something about friends, family, and culture. they say something about why we live as we do from year to year.

psychotherapy job#

  • help client craft professional stories about themselves that make sense, stories they can take around to job interviews. personal stories are trickier. our most intimate self-defining memories can be pieced together in strange, even painful ways.
