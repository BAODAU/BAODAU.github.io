Ah Here We Go Again

Posted Jun 12, 2019

Hello world.

Hi, I’m going to try to blog again. From my experience from past attempts, I can’t keep up with writing essays if I only treat it as a ‘feels good’ activity when I procrastinate. Now that I know better, I will actually put self-imposed deadlines to churn out content for this blog.

Who knows what to make of this decision. I’m in part hopeful that I will try to do my research and write interesting articles.

Or I might half-ass my way to meet the deadlines. That’s more likely the case,but hey, at least you guys know that I tried and failed and laugh at me.

Anyway, I’m thinking of structuring my blog like this:

Most of it will be personal.

Some will be technical.

I will sprinkle some juicy book reviews every now and then.

At the time of writing, I’m thinking of incorporating a commenting system. It will allow everybody on technical essays and only my close friends and my family on personal posts.

I hope this blog will outlive me and reach further than I physically could.

Ah let’s do this together!
